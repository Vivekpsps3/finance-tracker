# Innovation And Differentiated Value

## Current Findings

`INNO-001` through `INNO-003` are experiments gated by `SEC-001`, `COR-001`, `COR-002`, `COR-003`, and `DOC-001`.

## Target State

`LocalFinancialSnapshot` feeds deterministic versioned `FinancialSignal` detectors. Signals state fact, inference, or scenario; cite encrypted local evidence; carry confidence; offer reversible action; and store feedback encrypted. Observed snapshots precede change attribution. Stock Lab and planning expose assumptions and sensitivity instead of authoritative predictions.

## Acceptance Criteria

No proposal sends private financial inputs or mutates truth. Experiments have measurable success criteria, false-positive handling, dismissals, deterministic fixtures, and network-zero assertions. Brokerage import/reconciliation stays client-side unless a separately approved disclosure boundary changes.

## Verification

Measure fixture precision/recall, completion outcomes, and privacy regression coverage before wider release.
