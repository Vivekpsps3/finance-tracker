# SpaceX Themed Revamp Progress

## Goal

Revamp the finance tracker with a subtle but clearly SpaceX-inspired interface: dark orbital surfaces, white typography, restrained red accents, launch/mission language, and technical panel styling without turning the app into a novelty theme.

## Principles

- Keep finance workflows dense, legible, and practical.
- Apply the theme globally through tokens and shared primitives first.
- Use SpaceX-inspired language and visual motifs sparingly: mission control, launch, orbit, telemetry, payload, and trajectory.
- Avoid breaking routes, data flows, or tests while changing presentation.
