Design section 1: Product scope

This page is a speculative planning workbench, not a portfolio mutation surface. It analyzes explicit typed symbols and existing owned symbols, accepts ticker disclosure to the backend/yfinance, saves encrypted scenario inputs, and never writes holdings, assets, liabilities, transactions, or net worth.
